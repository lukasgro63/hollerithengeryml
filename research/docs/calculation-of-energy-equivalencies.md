# Calculation of Energy Equivalencies

We convert emissions or energy data to the equivalent amount of carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions from using that amount. The calculation helps translate abstract measurements into concrete terms you can understand, such as the annual emissions from cars, households, or power plants.

## Input Data

We can calculate the Equivalencies by the following data:
- Carbon Dioxide or CO2 Equivalent
- Carbon
- Kilowatt-hours used
